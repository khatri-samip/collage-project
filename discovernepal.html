Adventure Overview

Nepal offers some of the most diverse adventure activities in the world. Whether you’re a trekker, a thrill-seeker, or a nature lover, there’s something here for everyone.



Most Popular Adventure Activities

Trekking

Explore iconic trails like Everest Base Camp, Annapurna Circuit, and Langtang Valley, experiencing mountains, culture, and wildlife.

Whitewater Rafting

Ride the thrilling rapids of Bhote Koshi, Trishuli, and Seti rivers, surrounded by breathtaking Himalayan landscapes.

Bungee Jump

Leap off the iconic Bhote Koshi suspension bridge, the highest bungee jump in Nepal, for an adrenaline-pumping freefall over the gorge.

Zipline

Fly across valleys and rivers on some of the world’s longest and highest ziplines, enjoying panoramic Himalayan views.

Paragliding

Soar over Pokhara with breathtaking views of Phewa Lake, the Annapurna range, and the entire Pokhara valley from the sky.

Mountain Biking

Ride trails through hills, forests, and villages in Pokhara, Kathmandu Valley, and Mustang for an adventurous biking experience.

Rock Climbing

Challenge yourself on natural rock faces in Shivapuri, Chisapani, and Pokhara cliffs with breathtaking mountain backdrops.

Wildlife Safari

Jeep and elephant safaris in Chitwan or Bardia National Parks to see rhinos, tigers, elephants, and exotic birds in their natural habitat.

Canyoning

Descend waterfalls, rapids, and gorges in the Himalayan valleys for a mix of trekking, jumping, swimming, and abseiling.

Camping & Nature Hikes

Experience remote landscapes, serene rivers, and forests while camping under the stars along trails and valleys in Nepal.

Jungle Trekking

Explore subtropical jungles in Chitwan, Bardia, and Suklaphanta while spotting wildlife and indigenous communities.

Ice Climbing

Climb frozen waterfalls and ice walls in regions like Langtang and Annapurna for a thrilling winter adventure.

Rock Scrambling

Navigate rocky terrains of the Himalayan foothills combining trekking and climbing skills for an adventurous challenge.

Kayaking

Experience rivers like Seti and Bhote Koshi on kayaks, combining whitewater thrills and scenic beauty.

Hot Air Ballooning

Soar over the Kathmandu Valley or Chitwan National Park at sunrise for peaceful aerial views of mountains and jungles.

Trail Running

Run through scenic trails in Pokhara, Annapurna, and Shivapuri hills while challenging yourself physically and mentally.

Skydiving

Experience freefall over Pokhara valley and lakes with spectacular aerial views of the Himalayas.

Horse Riding

Enjoy scenic horseback rides in Mustang, Annapurna, and Pokhara valleys while exploring traditional villages.

Fishing

Relax along rivers and lakes in Nepal, catching trout and other freshwater species while enjoying peaceful nature.

Waterfall Adventure

Discover hidden waterfalls and natural rock pools in Pokhara and Langtang for swimming, climbing, and relaxation.

Cultural Adventure

Combine trekking and exploration with visits to monasteries, temples, and traditional festivals to immerse yourself in Nepalese culture.

Rock & Ice Exploration

Adventure through glaciers, ice caves, and rock formations in the Himalayas for an extreme adventure experience.
