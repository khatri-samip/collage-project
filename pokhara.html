Pokhara

The scenic city by Phewa Lake, gateway to the Annapurna region, famous for adventure and natural beauty.

Natural Wonders & Attractions

Phewa Lake: Serene atmosphere, boating with reflections of the Annapurna range.

Tal Barahi Temple: Two-story pagoda temple on an island in the lake.

Sarangkot: Sunrise and paragliding viewpoint with Himalayan panorama.

World Peace Pagoda: Hilltop Buddhist stupa with panoramic views.

Davis Falls: Waterfall disappearing into an underground tunnel.

Gupteshwor Mahadev Cave: Sacred limestone cave opposite Davis Falls.

Seti River Gorge: Deep gorge cutting through the city center.

Adventure & Activities

Paragliding: World-class paragliding from Sarangkot.

Ultra-light Flights: Small aircraft offering close views of mountains.

Trekking: Starting point for Annapurna and Poon Hill treks.

Culture & History

International Mountain Museum: Dedicated to Himalayan mountaineering and people.

Bindhyabasini Temple: Historic Hindu temple with city views.
