Rara Lake — The Queen of Lakes

Largest lake in Nepal, located in remote Mugu District within Rara National Park, famous for its pristine blue waters and wilderness.

Essential Facts

FeatureDetail
LocationMugu District, Karnali Province, Western Nepal
National ParkRara National Park (Nepal's smallest)
Altitude (Elevation)Approx. 2,990 m (9,810 ft)
SizeMax length 5 km, max width 3 km, area ~10.8 sq km
DepthMaximum 167 m (548 ft)
SignificanceLargest lake in Nepal, often called the "Queen of Lakes"

Trekking to Rara Lake

Difficulty: Moderate. Remote trails with steep ascents and descents. Duration: 12–16 days from Kathmandu, shorter via connecting flights.

Best Time to Visit

Autumn (Sept–Nov): Mild, clear skies.
Spring (Mar–May): Blooming rhododendrons, warmer days.
Winter (Dec–Feb): Very cold with snow.
Monsoon (Jun–Aug): Heavy rain, slippery trails.

Required Permits

  • Rara National Park Entry Permit
  • TIMS (Trekkers' Information Management System) Card

Flora & Fauna

Flora: Lower elevations have Blue Pine, Rhododendrons, Himalayan spruce, juniper, and oak. Higher elevations feature fir, spruce, and pine.

CategoryNotable Species
MammalsMusk Deer, Himalayan Black Bear, Leopard, Red Panda (rare), Himalayan Tahr, Wild Dog, Common Langur, Rhesus Macaque
BirdsOver 200 species including Coots, Snow Cock, Pheasants, Mallards, and migratory waterfowls
Aquatic LifeThree endemic fish species, found nowhere else

Activities & Scenic Highlights

  • Murma Top Hike: 2-hour uphill hike to Chuchemara Peak (~4,039 m) for panoramic lake views
  • Boating/Paddling: Hire local wooden boats on the calm waters
  • Circumambulation (Lake Walk): Explore the lake shoreline
  • Horse Riding: Offered by local villagers near the lake and Murma Village

Travel & Logistics

Mode of TransportRoute / Duration
FastestKathmandu → Nepalgunj (1 hr) → Talcha Airport (30–45 min) → Trek to lake (2–3 hrs)
Standard TrekKathmandu → Nepalgunj → Jumla Airport → Trek to lake (4–7 days)
OverlandLong jeep journey (2–4 days) to nearest roadhead, then trek to lake

Facilities are basic; teahouses or lodges are simple. Carry cash, warm clothing, and first aid kit.
