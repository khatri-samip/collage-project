Momo

Delicious Nepali dumplings, steamed or fried, filled with meat or vegetables. A must-try street food across Nepal.

History and Origin

Momo originated from Tibet and was introduced to Nepal by Tibetan immigrants. It has become one of Nepal's most popular street foods.

Types and Fillings

Steamed Momo: Traditional, soft, and juicy.

Fried Momo: Crispy and golden brown.

Fillings: Chicken, buffalo, pork, vegetables, cheese, or a combination.

Serving and Accompaniments

Served hot with sesame or tomato-based achar (spicy dipping sauce). Often enjoyed with chili sauce and pickled vegetables.

Fun Facts

Momo is so popular in Nepal that there are momo festivals and competitions. Street vendors and restaurants serve thousands every day. Vegetarian versions are widely available.
